Project Overview

shifto.com is a packers and movers aggregator built to simplify relocations. The platform captures enquiries from multiple sources, uses algorithmic and bidding logic to assign orders to vendors, and provides tools for call-centres, reporting and reconciliation.

Built with PHP 8 and Laravel 8 on MySQL 8, the portal focuses on reliability, quick vendor matching, and clear operational workflows that improve conversion rates and reduce manual overhead for operations teams.

Role Overview — Team Lead / Lead Developer

As the technical lead for the project I drove the architecture and development of the Movers & Packers Integration Portal, led module delivery, and coordinated with operations and support to ensure the platform met business SLAs and operational needs.

Important Features / Modules

  • Order Capturing: Centralized ingestion of leads and enquiries from multiple channels with de-duplication and routing rules.
  • Item & Category Management: Structured item definitions to improve cost estimation and vendor quote accuracy.
  • Bidding & Assignment Engine: Rule-based matching and vendor bidding to optimize cost and SLA.
  • Calling Management: Agent workflows, click-to-call, call logs and outcomes tracking for better conversions.
  • Reporting & Analytics: Dashboard for orders, vendor performance, revenue and operational metrics.
  • Web Services: API & Webhook integrations for partners, payment gateway and external systems.
  • Vendor Portal: Vendors receive leads, submit quotes, accept jobs and update statuses via a dedicated dashboard.

Impact & Outcomes

  • Centralized enquiry handling reduced lead loss and improved vendor response times.
  • Bidding engine increased competitive pricing and lowered average fulfillment cost for customers.
  • Calling workflows and agent tooling improved first-contact closure rates and NPS.
  • Reporting enabled data-driven vendor management and SLA enforcement.

Future Enhancements
